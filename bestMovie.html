Best Movie

Title: Now You See Me

Link to Movie Info

Why I Like the Movie

I like this movie because the effects are very well done and during and after the movie you are constantly amazed. The movie itself is about magicians pulling off amazing international tricks that help families that were ripped off by an insurance company. The four horseman then try and join The Eye. The movie itself is so mind boggling, it is a work of magic itself.

Best Actor/Actress

The best actress is Isla Fisher who plays Henley Reeves. In the movie she is a self made magician that used to be one of the other character's assistant. She is very funny and an independent woman in the movie. However, she does not appear in the sequel.
